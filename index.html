About Us

Welcome to MM Pets, where passion for exotic animals meets exceptional care. We specialize in ethically sourcing and raising unique companions including Sugar Gliders, Snakes, Cats, and Birds.

Our mission is to provide healthy, well-socialized pets and the expert advice you need to give them a thriving life. Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or a first-time exotic pet owner, we are here to support your journey.
